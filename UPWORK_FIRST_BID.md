## Key Tips for the First Bid

1. **ALWAYS offer a free sample first** — this removes the client's risk
2. **Keep it under 200 words** — clients skim, they don't read
3. **Mention "free sample" in the first 2 sentences** — highest attention
4. **Never say "I'm new" or "I'm cheap"** — say "competitive rates" instead
5. **End with a question** — forces the client to reply

## Jobs to Target First

Sort Upwork by "Newest" and filter:
- Category: Data Entry / Web Scraping
- Budget: $10-$50
- Less than 5 proposals submitted

These are your sweet spot for the first 5 jobs.
