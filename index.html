FAQ

Common Questions

What websites can you scrape?

Any publicly accessible website. E-commerce sites, directories, real estate platforms, job boards, social media (public data only). I follow each site's robots.txt and legal terms.

How long does a typical project take?

Small projects (under 500 items): 1-2 days. Medium projects (500-5000 items): 2-4 days. Large projects: timeline discussed case by case.

What format do I get the data in?

CSV (opens in Excel/Google Sheets), Excel (.xlsx), JSON, or any format you need. Data is cleaned, deduplicated, and ready to use.

Is web scraping legal?

Scraping publicly available data is generally legal. I only scrape public data, respect robots.txt, and never access login-protected content without authorization.
